2. [[Perfection of Wisdom/Topics/Conceptions of apprehenders (Seeing)\|Conceptions of apprehenders]] མཐོང་སྤང་ཤེས་སྒྲིབ་འཛིན་རྟོག

**Possibilities of the conceptions:** (by way of objects)
- *Apprehendeds but not Apprehenders:* Adherence to forms and so forth as truly existing objects of utilisation  གཟུགས་སོགས་ལ་ལོངས་སྤྱད་བྱར་བདེན་པར་ཞེན་པ།
  (Not the latter because it is not an object-possessor)
- *Apprehenders but not Apprehendeds:* Adherence to a single moment of consciousness as a truly existing utiliser ཤེས་པ་སྐད་ཅིག་གཅིག་ལ་ལོངས་སྤྱོད་པ་པོར་བདེན་པར་ཞེན་པ།
  (Not the latter because it cannot be realised by ordinary beings' direct perception 
  སོ་སོ་སྐྱེ་བོའི་མངོན་སུམ་གྱིས་རྟོགས་མི་ནུས་པ།)
- *Both:* A continuous consciousness རྒྱུན་དང་ལྡན་པའི་ཤེས་པ།
	- Adherence to that as an object of utilisation is the conception of apprehendeds
	- Adherence to that as a utiliser is the conception of apprehenders
- *Neither:* Non-dual perfection of wisdom མངོན་གཉིས་སུ་མེད་པའི་ཤེར་ཕྱིན།
  e.g. exalted knower of all aspects རྣམ་མཁྱེན། 
  Not an object of either conception because:
	- Cannot be directly realised by ordinary beings' direct perception
	- Does not exist in by ordinary beings' continua

Therefore, in this context (Vimuktasena's Illumination), this is in relation to ordinary beings:
- Being an apprehended depends on being realised or not by ordinary beings' direct perception
  e.g. five sense objects
- Being an apprehender depends on existing in the continua of ordinary beings
  e.g. a single moment of consciousness
