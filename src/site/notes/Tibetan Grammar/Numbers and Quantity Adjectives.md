These are numbers and adjectives describing quantity.

**Illustrations:** solitary (གཅིག་པུ།) many (མང་པོ།) some (ཉུང་ཤས།) a little (ཅུང་ཟད།)

If used together with other common adjectives, then they will usually be affixed at the end, for example:
Three big red apples (ཀུ་ཤུ་དམར་པོ་ཆེན་པོ་གསུམ།)

However, there can be differences in emphasis:
ཁང་པ་དམར་པོ་དང་པོ། vs ཁང་པ་དང་པོ་དམར་པོ།
“The first red house” (out of many red houses) vs “The first house, which is red”
